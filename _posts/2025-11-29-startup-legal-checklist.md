# Startup Legal Checklist for Founders

Har startup ko shuru me ye legal points ready rakhne chahiye:

## Company Setup
- **Private Limited** registration
- Founders **equity split** & ESOP policy

## Compliance
- GST / PAN / Professional Tax
- Accounting & ROC filings

## Intellectual Property
- **Trademark** for brand name & logo
- Founders **IP Assignment Agreement**

## Contracts
- Founders Agreement
- Vendor & Service contracts
- NDA before funding & discussions
