For decades, we have stood at the confluence of tradition and truth. Today, we stand at the new frontier: the intersection of constitutional values and digital reality.

2010

The Foundation

Adv. Ganga Shankar establishes the chambers at Civil Lines, Allahabad. Initially focused on Constitutional Writs and Criminal Defense during a turbulent era in Uttar Pradesh's legal history.

2011

Expansion to the Apex

Following a series of high-profile acquittals, the firm opens its Delhi chapter to handle Special Leave Petitions (SLPs) at the Supreme Court of India.

2018

The Digital Pivot

Recognizing the Puttaswamy Judgment (Right to Privacy) as a watershed moment, JusticeFirms establishes its dedicated "Cyber & Tech" wing led by the new generation of partners.

2026

JusticeFirms 2.0

Full integration of AI-assisted discovery and the launch of the "Policy Innovation Lab" to navigate the BNS/BNSS transition.
